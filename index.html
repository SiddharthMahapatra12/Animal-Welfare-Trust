    About Us

    An Initiative by a group of animal activists to bring a change in animal welfare in Bhubaneswar.
    Created by Animal Lovers, for aniamls, we are for the Welfare of Animals in Odisha and aim for all Animals in India by collaboration.

    Our Mission

    An organization, a community of animal lovers, vegan and animal rights activists who look to respond to and care all Animals.

    Our Plan

    1. One Animal Rehabilitation centre.
    2. Animal rights activists.
    3. ABC and Vaccination programmes.
    4. Foster care network.
    5. Awareness/Education programs.

    Our Vision

    We aim to develop compassion for animals at a very root level and bring all form of casualties numbers to ZERO.
